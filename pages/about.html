What Is IPL?

The IPL Means Indian Premier League is an Indian professional Twenty20(T20)Cricket League established in 2008. Lalit Modi is the founder of IPL. Chris Gayle holds the record for scoring the fastest century in IPL history. Rajiv Mehta is the CEo of the IPL
