India vs South Africa Final

India's cricket team emerged victorious at the T20 World Cup 2024, defeating South Africa by a narrow margin of 7 runs in a gripping final at Barbados' Kensington Oval. This win not only secured India's second T20 World Cup title but also marked their first ICC trophy in 11 years, setting off jubilant celebrations on Saturday, June 29. Following the victory, Rahul Dravid, known for his composed demeanor, let his emotions flow freely as India secured their second ICC T20 World Cup title after an 11-year gap. After the intense trophy presentation, where Rohit Sharma and Virat Kohli celebrated on stage, Kohli ensured Dravid was brought into the spotlight.
